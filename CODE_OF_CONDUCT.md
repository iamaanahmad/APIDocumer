# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Focus on ideas and code, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith participation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through repository issues or private maintainer contact channels.

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.
